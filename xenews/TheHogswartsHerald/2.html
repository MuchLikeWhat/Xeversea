Nimbus 2000 Discovered Hovering Above Indonesia’s South Sea

SOUTH SEA COAST, INDONESIA – In a development as astonishing as the earlier discovery of Dobby the free house-elf, a second magical anomaly has been confirmed along the shores of Pantai Laut Selatan.

On the evening of the incident, several beachgoers reported witnessing what they initially believed to be “a stick suspended by invisible string.” As dusk settled, however, the object was seen clearly against the fading light: a polished racing broom, hovering nearly two metres above the sand, entirely riderless.

Magical authorities later identified the broom — through residual enchantment tracing — as a Nimbus 2000 , a model famously flown at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during earlier Quidditch seasons.

Seven Minutes of Silence

According to eyewitness accounts, the broom remained suspended for precisely seven minutes. It neither advanced nor retreated, but hovered as if awaiting instruction. One witness claimed the air around it shimmered faintly, and the tide momentarily stilled in an unnatural calm

Moments before its disappearance, the broom reportedly tilted forward — the unmistakable posture of a racing launch — and shot upwards into the darkening sky, vanishing without sound.

When representatives from Kementerian Sihir Nusantara arrived, they discovered concentrated traces of flight-magic and transcontinental displacement energy identical to that detected at the site where Dobby had been found days earlier.

A Message — or a Malfunction?

Scholars are divided on the purpose of the manifestation.

Some theorise the broom may have been caught in a disrupted Portkey current linking Britain and Indonesia. Others suggest something far more deliberate — that the broom’s appearance was a signal, perhaps even a test of a magical pathway.

A senior British Ministry official commented cautiously:

“Nimbus brooms do not simply wander across oceans. If this was intentional, it implies coordination — or interference — at a very high magical level."

The Maitreyawira Alignment

Investigators mapping magical residue across Palembang have uncovered a curious alignment: the coordinates of the broom’s hovering location correspond almost precisely with ley-line intersections beneath Maitreyawira School.

Though no formal declaration has been made, whispers within the Department of Mysteries suggest that Maitreyawira may sit upon a dormant conduit — one possibly resonant with Hogwarts’ own ancient enchantments.

Students in the surrounding area have reportedly experienced brief electrical disruptions and fleeting shadows resembling winged shapes overhead. While such claims remain unverified, authorities are monitoring the situation closely.

Echoes Across the Sea

The Nimbus 2000’s appearance marks the second confirmed magical occurrence on Indonesia’s southern coast within a fortnight. Combined with the prior tragedy involving Dobby, speculation is mounting that the South Sea may have become an unintended crossroads of wizarding energies.

For now, both British and Indonesian magical officials maintain that the International Statute of Secrecy remains secure. Obliviators have reportedly attended to all necessary witnesses.

Yet among those who study ancient magic, one question lingers:

If a broom can cross continents unaided…
What else might follow?

The Hogwarts Herald continues its investigation into the growing connection between Indonesia’s South Sea and the hidden corridors of Hogwarts.
