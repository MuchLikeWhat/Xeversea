International Magical Incident – Urgent Report

Dobby the House-Elf Discovered on Indonesia’s South Sea Coast

Muggle Witnesses Report Unusual Magical Phenomena

HOGWARTS, SCOTLAND – In a shocking international development, the body of Dobby, the free house-elf and known ally of Harry Potter, has reportedly been discovered along the southern coastline of Indonesia, commonly referred to by locals as Pantai Laut Selatan.

The discovery was made by a group of Indonesian secondary school girls during what appeared to be a coastal excursion. Witnesses allegedly described finding “a small humanoid creature with large ears” lying upon the sand shortly after an unexplained magical disturbance over the sea.

Transported Across Continents

Sources within the Ministry of Magic suggest that Dobby may have been forcibly transported thousands of miles from Britain during the escalating activities of He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named. Magical residue detected along the shoreline indicates advanced Dark magic consistent with long-distance forced Apparition or magical displacement.

One senior official from the Department of International Magical Cooperation stated:

“Such a displacement would require extraordinary power. It is deeply concerning that this incident occurred outside British jurisdiction.”

Breach of Secrecy in Indonesia

The situation has become particularly delicate after reports surfaced that the Muggle students began researching their discovery online. According to insiders, several of them reportedly encountered references to “Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry” through obscure magical interference in digital systems.

The Indonesian Ministry of Magic (known locally as Kementerian Sihir Nusantara) is said to be working urgently with British authorities to contain the breach. Obliviators have been dispatched, though it is believed that at least a few fragments of memory may remain.

A confidential report suggests that one of the girls was overheard saying:

“If this is real… then Hogwarts is real too.”

International Wizarding Response

Hogwarts staff have declined to comment publicly, though students confirm that a memorial has been established near the kitchens, where Dobby once worked willingly as a paid elf.

Candlelight tributes have also been reported along Indonesia’s South Sea coast, where local magical communities are said to be quietly honouring Dobby’s bravery.

Though the Statute of Secrecy remains officially intact, this unprecedented cross-continental incident has raised concerns among wizarding authorities worldwide.

One thing remains beyond dispute:

Dobby died free.
And in death, he may have bridged two worlds.

The Hogwarts Herald will continue monitoring this developing story.

International Magical Investigation

Nimbus 2000 Discovered Hovering Above Indonesia’s South Sea

SOUTH SEA COAST, INDONESIA – In a development as astonishing as the earlier discovery of Dobby the free house-elf, a second magical anomaly has been confirmed along the shores of Pantai Laut Selatan.

On the evening of the incident, several beachgoers reported witnessing what they initially believed to be “a stick suspended by invisible string.” As dusk settled, however, the object was seen clearly against the fading light: a polished racing broom, hovering nearly two metres above the sand, entirely riderless.

Magical authorities later identified the broom — through residual enchantment tracing — as a Nimbus 2000 , a model famously flown at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during earlier Quidditch seasons.

Seven Minutes of Silence

According to eyewitness accounts, the broom remained suspended for precisely seven minutes. It neither advanced nor retreated, but hovered as if awaiting instruction. One witness claimed the air around it shimmered faintly, and the tide momentarily stilled in an unnatural calm

Moments before its disappearance, the broom reportedly tilted forward — the unmistakable posture of a racing launch — and shot upwards into the darkening sky, vanishing without sound.

When representatives from Kementerian Sihir Nusantara arrived, they discovered concentrated traces of flight-magic and transcontinental displacement energy identical to that detected at the site where Dobby had been found days earlier.

A Message — or a Malfunction?

Scholars are divided on the purpose of the manifestation.

Some theorise the broom may have been caught in a disrupted Portkey current linking Britain and Indonesia. Others suggest something far more deliberate — that the broom’s appearance was a signal, perhaps even a test of a magical pathway.

A senior British Ministry official commented cautiously:

“Nimbus brooms do not simply wander across oceans. If this was intentional, it implies coordination — or interference — at a very high magical level."

The Maitreyawira Alignment

Investigators mapping magical residue across Palembang have uncovered a curious alignment: the coordinates of the broom’s hovering location correspond almost precisely with ley-line intersections beneath Maitreyawira School.

Though no formal declaration has been made, whispers within the Department of Mysteries suggest that Maitreyawira may sit upon a dormant conduit — one possibly resonant with Hogwarts’ own ancient enchantments.

Students in the surrounding area have reportedly experienced brief electrical disruptions and fleeting shadows resembling winged shapes overhead. While such claims remain unverified, authorities are monitoring the situation closely.

Echoes Across the Sea

The Nimbus 2000’s appearance marks the second confirmed magical occurrence on Indonesia’s southern coast within a fortnight. Combined with the prior tragedy involving Dobby, speculation is mounting that the South Sea may have become an unintended crossroads of wizarding energies.

For now, both British and Indonesian magical officials maintain that the International Statute of Secrecy remains secure. Obliviators have reportedly attended to all necessary witnesses.

Yet among those who study ancient magic, one question lingers:

If a broom can cross continents unaided…
What else might follow?

The Hogwarts Herald continues its investigation into the growing connection between Indonesia’s South Sea and the hidden corridors of Hogwarts.

Forest Omen

Centaur Apparition in Indonesian Rainforest Deepens Mystery

SUMATRA, INDONESIA – As investigations into the South Sea anomalies continue, a third extraordinary incident has emerged — this time not by the shore, but deep within Indonesia’s rainforest.

Local villagers reported sighting a towering figure at twilight: the upper body of a man with the powerful frame of a horse below. The being stood in a forest clearing beneath a rising crescent moon, unmoving, silent, and gazing intently at the stars.

Magical experts have confirmed that the description matches that of a Centaur, a sentient magical being traditionally residing within the Forbidden Forest near Hogwarts.

A Scholar of the Skies

Centaurs are not known to travel casually, nor do they interfere with wizarding affairs without purpose. Renowned for their mastery of astronomy and interpretation of celestial signs, they observe rather than act — and when they do appear, it is rarely without meaning.

One witness claimed the creature traced patterns in the soil with the tip of a carved wooden spear before retreating into the mist without sound. No signs of aggression were recorded.

Officials from Kementerian Sihir Nusantara conducted a sweep of the area and detected faint but unmistakable traces of British forest-magic signature — the same resonance pattern linked to Hogwarts grounds.

Celestial Alignment

Astronomical records reveal that on the night of the sighting, several constellations traditionally studied by Centaur clans were in rare alignment — including Mars and Saturn in visible opposition.

A Hogwarts professor of Divination, speaking anonymously, remarked:

“Centaurs read the heavens as we read books. If one has journeyed so far from Britain, the sky must have written something urgent indeed.”

The Expanding Pattern

When mapped geographically, the rainforest clearing lies along the same ley-line trajectory connecting the South Sea coastline and Maitreyawira School in Palembang.

This marks the third magical manifestation in Indonesia within a short span:

  • The tragic displacement of Dobby.
  • The hovering Nimbus 2000.
  • And now, a silent Centaur beneath foreign stars.

Scholars now speculate whether an ancient conduit between Southeast Asia and Hogwarts has reawakened — not through spellwork, but through cosmic alignment.

Watching the Horizon

No further sightings have been confirmed. However, patrol charms have been strengthened around key magical nodes in the region. Hogwarts itself has neither confirmed nor denied internal disturbances matching these events.

If the Centaur came seeking answers in the heavens, then perhaps the answer is yet to unfold.

For in matters of prophecy, silence often speaks louder than spells.

The Hogwarts Herald remains vigilant as the mystery surrounding Indonesia’s growing magical convergence continues to deepen.

Artefact Anomaly

The Marauder’s Map Surfaces in Indonesia — Then Vanishes Without Trace

SOUTH SEA COAST, INDONESIA – In what may prove to be the most alarming development yet, an enchanted parchment believed to resemble the legendary Marauder’s Map was reportedly discovered along the same stretch of Pantai Laut Selatan where previous magical disturbances occurred.

The artefact was found at low tide by early-morning visitors. At first glance, it appeared to be an ordinary, weathered sheet of parchment. However, witnesses described ink lines shifting across its surface — forming corridors, staircases, and labelled names that moved as though alive.

One observer recalled faint words appearing briefly across the top of the parchment before fading.

Instantaneous Disappearance

Moments after one of the finders touched the document, the parchment vanished entirely. No ash. No residue. Only a slight warmth in the air and sand disturbed beneath where it had rested.

Magical investigators later confirmed traces of concealment enchantments consistent with defensive spells designed to protect highly sensitive magical artefacts.

If authentic, the Marauder’s Map is no ordinary object. Created by four Hogwarts students — Messrs Moony, Wormtail, Padfoot and Prongs — the map is capable of revealing every passage, secret corridor, and moving individual within Hogwarts Castle.

Its sudden manifestation thousands of miles from Scotland raises profound concerns.

A Deliberate Warning?

The Department of Mysteries has reportedly taken interest in the timing of the event. The map’s appearance occurred precisely nine days after the Nimbus 2000 sighting and under similar tidal conditions to the night Dobby’s body was discovered.

Such repetition suggests pattern rather than coincidence.

An unnamed British official stated:

“The Map does not simply drift across oceans. It responds to intent. If it appeared, someone — or something — meant it to be seen.”

The Maitreyawira Hypothesis Strengthens

All three manifestations — the Nimbus 2000, the Centaur sighting, and now the Marauder’s Map — align geographically with subterranean magical currents beneath Maitreyawira School in Palembang.

Advanced rune-analysis conducted jointly by British and Indonesian authorities indicates that the school grounds may rest upon an ancient ley-line convergence point — one potentially synchronised with Hogwarts’ foundational enchantments laid centuries ago.

Some scholars cautiously propose the existence of a dormant “Mirror Gate” — a magical resonance phenomenon in which two distant locations share structural enchantment patterns, allowing objects and beings to cross unintentionally when magical equilibrium is disturbed.

No official confirmation has been issued.

Four Incidents. One Convergence.

  • Dobby’s tragic displacement to Indonesia’s South Sea.
  • The hovering Nimbus 2000.
  • A silent Centaur beneath Sumatra’s stars.
  • The fleeting reappearance of the Marauder’s Map.

Each event grows less accidental than the last.

Whether these manifestations represent a warning, a fracture in magical boundaries, or the awakening of a long-forgotten alliance between Hogwarts and a distant institution remains uncertain.

But one conclusion now seems unavoidable:

Indonesia’s South Sea is no longer merely a shoreline.
It has become a threshold.

The Hogwarts Herald will continue its coverage as authorities on two continents seek answers hidden between tide and starlight.
