About Xeversea

Xeversea is a student class at Maitreyawira Palembang School, dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration among students interested in technology and design. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore their passions, develop their skills, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Founded in 2025, Xeversea has grown into a vibrant community of students who are passionate about technology, design, and innovation. We organize various events, workshops, and projects that allow our members to learn, create, and share their ideas with others.

Our activities include coding workshops, design challenges, hackathons, and collaborative projects that encourage creativity and teamwork. We also provide opportunities for our members to showcase their work and connect with industry professionals through events and partnerships.

At Xeversea, we believe in the power of collaboration and the importance of fostering a supportive and inclusive community. We welcome students of all backgrounds and skill levels to join us in our journey of exploration and innovation.
